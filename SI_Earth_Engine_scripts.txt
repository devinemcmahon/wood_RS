Earth Engine scripts to identify current (2016) Eucalyptus plantations
	and extract time series of vegetation indices (1984-2016) for each
	plantation stand and its adjacent native vegetation
These scripts should be available to all Earth Engine users with the links.

Create and export training data for a land cover classfier:
https://code.earthengine.google.com/db50cc37b45b7f797438ffd853fb259f

Classify land cover in a Landsat scene and test the accuracy:
https://code.earthengine.google.com/05eec64f8a3f64bbbf8da1d770af5206

Export classified Eucalyptus stands as a KML file:
https://code.earthengine.google.com/404877d389d80edfd5536ecfdfbc007d

Export classified native vegetation patches around stands:
https://code.earthengine.google.com/9beeca40c3ca76bce4ae6d980c47a5ef

Export a time series of NIRv observations for each stand and patch:
https://code.earthengine.google.com/b03bf7cf4548afe4c6b56f067e793b6e
	
Add properties (e.g. area, elevation) to a stand or patch:
https://code.earthengine.google.com/5442e00faa50a8a4ea34ecb9a59e0fee

Add meterological data to an area containing stands and patches:
https://code.earthengine.google.com/83823dc25d1d3d17f979d6de0f491536

Fusion table of all the training polygons:
https://www.google.com/fusiontables/DataSource?docid=1jhdlA4KZKQ3sAo0PsKNU0QyQS-AtcD53mTiM5UCl
